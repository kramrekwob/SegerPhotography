About

I’m Lex!

I’m a Brooklyn based photographer originally from Westchester, NY.

When you look at a photo, there is no person in the world that interprets it the exact same way as you, not even the photographer. It may spark a memory, a feeling or an idea that inspires you in some way.

My goal is to capture that fleeting moment that personally speaks to you in hopes that you escape from the everyday hustle even just for a moment. There is so much beauty to appreciate in this world, even if you don’t see it from your perspective right away.
